Shopt is a simple and efficient shopping list app that helps you organize your
shopping by grouping items by shop and ordering the items within each shop by
how you will walk by them.

Features:
• Group shopping items by shop for efficient shopping trips
• Check off items as you shop them
• Remove completed items with one tap
• Edit and manage your list of shops
• Edit and reorder items within each shop
• Custom item ordering matching your shopping route
• Clean, intuitive interface
• Works completely offline - no internet required
• No ads, no tracking, no unnecessary permissions

Perfect for anyone who wants a straight-forward shopping list app without
complexity or privacy concerns.
